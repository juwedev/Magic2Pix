::Gallery

Gallery of various pictures as portraits, fetish, street, people, landscape or products taken over several years. Please notice all pictures are subjects to © Copyright of the photographer. But for any use you can ask the photographer before. A written permission is required.

::Photo Location Finder

This feature is a test and affects the region of Nuremberg. It will suggest you various photo locations for different scenes with a compact description. For use you have to enable GPS in your device, otherwise the feature will not work. This feature calculates your location and the distance to the several photo shooting locations in the region of Nuremberg.

::Message

In this section you can send me directly a message without using an E-Mail client. This could be suggestions for improving this App or nice new photo locations you are missing in the 'Location Finder'. Or otherwise you need a photographer or only want to talk to me. Feel free to contact me :-)
